Definition and History of Mobile Banking

Mobile banking is the ability to make financial transactions using a mobile device, such as a smartphone or tablet. This form of banking has reduced customers' need to visit their bank's branches by making many financial services such as those mentioned above more accessible.

Advantages and Disadvantages of Mobile Banking

Mobile Banking offers numerous advantages such as 24/7 access to funds and account(s) and tools that assist with better financial management. However, it also has disadvantages like security issues and dependency on technology.

My Experience of Mobile Banking

Using mobile banking, I have done money transactions, applied for credit, paid my phone bills, and many more. Mobile banking makes getting these done faster than having to visit the physical branch to achieve these tasks.

Emerging Technology of Mobile Banking

Emerging technologies in mobile banking are revolutionizing how financial services are delivered. Innovations like AI, blockchain, biometric authentication, and 5G are driving mobile banking toward a secure and user-friendly future.
